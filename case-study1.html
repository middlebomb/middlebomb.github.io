B2B Platform

Telkomsel Business Partner Platform

Streamlining collaboration between a telecom giant and its partners through a unified digital ecosystem.

Role

UI/UX Designer

Timeline

4 Months

Tools

Figma, Miro, Jira

01. The Problem

Telkomsel's partners were using disparate systems to manage tickets and communications. This resulted in slow response times, data redundancy, and a confusing user experience. The goal was to centralize these workflows into a single, intuitive dashboard.

02. User Research

We conducted interviews with 15 account managers and 10 partner representatives. The key findings were:

  • Fragmented Data: Users had to open 4 different tabs to complete one task.
  • Lack of Status Updates: Partners never knew if their tickets were being processed.
  • Visual Clutter: The legacy system was text-heavy and hard to scan.

03. Ideation & Wireframes

Based on the research, I created low-fidelity wireframes focusing on a "Task-First" architecture. We prioritized the 'Ticket Status' and 'Quick Actions' on the dashboard.
